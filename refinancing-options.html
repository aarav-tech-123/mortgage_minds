Understanding the Refinancing Process

Refinancing a home loan involves several key steps — from analysing your existing mortgage to settling with a new lender. Understanding what's involved helps you make the right decision and avoid common pitfalls.

Key Steps When Refinancing a Home Loan

  • Review your current mortgage — know your outstanding balance, fixed term expiry, and any early repayment costs
  • Assess your financial position — review your income, expenses, and equity to understand what you qualify for
  • Compare lenders and rates — explore competitive home refinance interest rates across 20+ lenders
  • Apply for your new loan — complete your mortgage with the assistance of a Mortgage Minds adviser
  • Settle and save — your new loan is activated and your savings begin from day one
