Free Tools

Calculate Your Mortgage Repayments

Before you start searching for your first home, upgrade, or next investment property — know exactly how much you could repay and how much you can save by paying off your mortgage a little faster.

Using Our Calculator

Enter your loan amount and the repayment period — up to 30 years. Click calculate to see your weekly, fortnightly, and monthly repayments, plus the total amount you'll pay including interest.

Compare Home Loans

Easily compare various home loans by filling in your details to see how much interest you'd pay or save. See how rate changes affect your repayments so you can plan with confidence.

Get the Actual Figures

Get a solid idea of what you can afford and take the next step. Our mortgage advisers will look at your personal circumstances and aspirations to find you the best possible loan structure.

Choose Your Calculator

Select the tool that best suits what you'd like to work out.

Repayment Calculator

Enter your loan amount, interest rate, and term to calculate your weekly, fortnightly, and monthly repayment amounts — plus your total interest paid over the life of the loan.

Note: All figures are based on the assumption that inputs remain constant and are valid only at the time of entry. Calculated on a table-repayments term loan. Actual repayment amounts may vary slightly due to rounding or individual lender terms and conditions. Mortgage repayment is calculated assuming the stated interest rate remains constant throughout the loan period.

Loan Term Calculator
