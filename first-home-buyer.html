Understanding the First Home Buyer Mortgage Process

There are a few important steps that are involved when getting approved on your first mortgage. From assessing your borrowing power to arranging a deposit and obtaining loan pre-approval, then having professional help can help make this process easier.

Key Steps for First Home Buyers

  • Assess your borrowing capacity and budget
  • Build or organize your deposit (savings or KiwiSaver)
  • Compare the various lenders in mortgages.
  • Obtaining pre-approval is a good thing to do before looking at a property.
  • Finalize the mortgage and complete settlement

Types of First Time Home Buyer Loan Options

Various lenders will provide you with different solutions of loans based on your financial standing and the deposit amount. Knowledge of such options may enable you to select the appropriate mortgage structure.

Why Work With a First Home Mortgage Broker

It is hard to navigate the mortgage market alone and this can be time consuming and confusing. A first home mortgage broker acts as your advisor and introduces you to lenders who will fit your financial profile.

Benefits of working with a broker include:

A broker will make the banking process easier and can enable you to make informed decisions, as opposed approaching banks individually

Availability of several lenders and mortgage products.

Improved opportunities of getting good interest rates.

Professional assistance in the documentation and applications.

Assistance with mortgage pre-approval
